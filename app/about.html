Working Together

What I can do for you

I build websites and web applications. I can work on personal, professional, or commercial projects and ensure a beautiful and great experience on any screen size.

From the beginning, it is important that your project is carefully planned with the user in mind. Employing user centered design methods, I can make your final project enjoyable to use, efficient, and accessible to your targeted audience.

With my experience in coding and User Interface design, I can help you make your ideas a reality.

Working With Me

I can offer services to help plan your project, design your project, and then build and publish the final product. From the beginning we will work directly together to understand the goals of your project, the intended audience, and then any design specifics you need.

I will then design and present you with mockups or prototypes of what we came up with. We will not move on from the design phase until you are satisfied.

I will then go and build your project, and provide you with updates along the way so you are up to date with the progress of your project.

My Story...

I currently Live in Lexington Kentucky with my girlfriend Sarah and our cat Hunter (see adorable image). I grew up in Cincinnati, relocated to Lexington to attend school at the University of Kentucky, where I studied music performance (focus on percussion) and have been here ever since!

For years I worked as a Stage Manager and Live Sound Engineer for multiple venues in town. Wanting to be involved in music more directly I pursued music education, teaching private lessons and percussion at Middle Schools and High Schools in the Cincinnati area. While teaching, I still performed and picked up the occasional freelance gig, but eventually I wanted to shift out of music education into a new career. Back in college I had a mild interest in coding that never lead to anything at the time. I decided to dive back in, now having more time to research and learn about this exciting new field. Lowe and behold, a passion was born!

I immediately fell in love. I couldn't help but feel the joy for learning, creating, problem solving, and a deep affinity for coding languages and theory. I dove in head first taking online courses, going through tutorials, and basically absorbing as much material as possible. I have continued to learn, expand my skillset, and hone my craft ever since.

One of the most appealing aspects to coding is how broad the field is. There is so much to learn and so much mental stimulation and engagement that takes place every single day. It never gets boring, never gets old, is always new and exciting. Whether it be a new framework, file architecture, new language, or method of programming, everyday working in this field has brought something new, exciting, and fulfilling.

Made from scratch, by yours truly :)
