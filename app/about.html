I'm Tim!

My Story...

I currently Live in Lexington Kentucky with my girlfriend Sarah and our cat Hunter (see adorable images above). I grew up in Cincinnati, relocated to Lexington to attend school at the University of Kentucky, where I studied music performance (focus on percussion) and have stayed here ever since!

For years I worked as a stage manager and live sound Engineer, and eventually pursued music education, teaching private lessons and percussion at Middle Schools and High Schools in the Cincinnati area. While teaching, I still perform and pick up the occassional freelance gig, but eventually I wanted to shift out of music education into a new career. I started looking into coding out of curiousity that had surfaced and since subsided back in college. Lowe and behold a new passion was born!

I immediately fell in love. I couldn't help but feel joy for learning, creating, problem solving, and a deep affinity for coding languages and theory. I dove in head first taking online courses, going through tutorials, and basically absorbing as much material as possible.

I have continued to learn, expand my skillset and hone my craft ever since. One of the most appealing aspects to coding is the sheer amount of things to learn. It never gets boring, never gets old, is always new and exciting everyday because there is more to learn. Whether it be a new framework, file architecture, new language, or method of programming, everyday working in this field brings something new, exciting, and fulfilling.

Made from scratch, by yours truly :)
